Saint Francis Home Care Companies

Saint Francis Home Care companies offer a variety of services for those leaving the hospital to recover or manage an ongoing cardiac or other type of condition at home.

Saint Francis Home Health employs highly-trained and skilled staff members that provide services such as:

  • IV infusion
  • Wound and ostomy management
  • Medication management
  • Post stroke and neurological rehabilitation
  • Post amputation care
  • Speech, language and occupational therapy
  • Social work assessment and counseling

Saint Francis Hospice meets the physical, spiritual and psychological needs of the terminally ill patient. The patient and family are cared for using a multidisciplinary approach to services such as routine in-home care, nursing home care, respite care and, when needed, continuous care.

All Saints Home Medical is a partnership between Saint Francis Health System and St. John Medical Center that was created to provide medical equipment and infusion services to patients in the comfort of home. Services include the selection, delivery, set-up, education and maintenance of home medical equipment such as oxygen, continuous positive airway pressure (CPAP), beds, crutches, wheelchairs, as well as infusion therapies including antibiotics, anticoagulants, hydration, immunoglobulin, neupogen/epogen, pain control, IV steroids and total parenteral nutrition.
